From Nine to Eight: How Eris Changed the Solar System's Story

Undergraduate researcher Shamitaa Arora explores the transformative impact of Eris's discovery on planetary classification. Her analysis delves into the controversy surrounding Eris as a potential tenth planet, the International Astronomical Union's 2006 redefinition of a planet, and the eventual reclassification of Pluto as a dwarf planet. This study highlights the evolving nature of scientific definitions and their role in shaping our understanding of the solar system.
